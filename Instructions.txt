HOW TO RUN THIS PROJECT:

1. Open 'index.html' in any modern web browser (Chrome, Safari, Firefox, Edge).
2. You can do this by:
   - Double-clicking the 'index.html' file in your file explorer.
   - Right-clicking 'index.html' and selecting 'Open with...' then choosing your browser.
   - Dragging and dropping the 'index.html' file into an open browser window.

THEME TOGGLE:
- Use the animated switch in the top-right corner to toggle between Light and Dark modes.
- The state (Sun for Light, Moon for Dark) is animated and will persist even after you refresh the page.

INTERACTIVE STAGE:
- The background is a draggable infinite canvas.
- Click and drag anywhere (or use touch) to explore the space.
- Use the 'Recenter' button in the bottom-left to reset the view.
- Hover your mouse to see the grid-reveal effect.
